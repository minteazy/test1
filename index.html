Project Overview

Mint Eazy is a revolutionary platform designed to help people with big ideas and projects, but who may not have sufficient financial resources or professional teams to realize them. Mint Eazy provides simple and accessible tools to create your own crypto project, from token development to launching a website to attract investors.

The platform offers educational materials, step-by-step guides, and video tutorials to help you bring your ideas to life, even if you're just starting out in the world of cryptocurrency.

Project Goals

The mission of Mint Eazy is to democratize access to the cryptocurrency space by helping innovators with limited financial means gain access to the technology and resources that will allow them to realize their ideas.

Technological Advantages

Security: The MEZY token is designed using reliable security standards for smart contracts. This ensures protection against external manipulations and hacker attacks. In the future, the project may undergo external audits to further enhance user trust.

Decentralization and Transparency: By leveraging Binance Smart Chain, all transactions and operations within the project are transparent and decentralized, fostering greater community trust.

Scalability: We plan to integrate with other blockchains, allowing the project to operate not only on Binance Smart Chain but also on other platforms (through bridges and integrations).

Use Cases for the MEZY Token

  • Access to Educational Materials: MEZY tokens provide access to exclusive educational materials on the Mint Eazy platform.
  • Purchasing Consultancy Services: Token holders will be able to purchase personal consultations to help develop their projects.
  • Integration into Other Crypto Ecosystems: MEZY tokens can be used to participate in various decentralized applications (dApps) and other platforms integrated with the Mint Eazy project.
  • Rewards: In the future, tokens may be used for staking or to receive rewards for active participation in the project.

Tokenomics

The key instrument of the project is the MEZY token. The token is designed to ensure stability, protect against manipulation, and grant access to educational materials on the platform.

  • Maximum Supply: 100,000,000 MEZY.
  • Daily Unlock: 10,000 MEZY tokens are unlocked daily at 8:00 AM Alberta time (Canada).
  • Transaction Fee: 0.1% is charged from each transaction and goes to the contract owner.
  • Access to Educational Materials: Users holding at least 10,000 MEZY tokens will have access to exclusive educational materials, such as video tutorials and guides on token creation.
  • Token Locking After Purchase: Tokens will be locked for 33 days after purchase, helping to reduce volatility and protect the project from manipulation.

Protection Against Manipulation: With the gradual unlocking of tokens and a 33-day lock after purchase, the project is protected from large-scale price manipulation and significant market fluctuations.
